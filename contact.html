FAQ

Common Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Email us at info@lumenaautomation.co.in and we'll respond promptly.

What types of businesses do you work with?
We work with startups, SMEs, and large enterprises across industries. Whether you need cloud infrastructure, custom software, MDM fleet management, or a consumer SaaS product — our team is equipped to deliver at any scale.
How long does a typical project take?
Project timelines vary by scope. A cloud migration may take 4–8 weeks, while a custom SaaS platform can take 3–6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation phase after understanding your requirements.
Is BudgetManager free to use?
BudgetManager offers both free and premium tiers. Core budgeting features are available for free, while advanced analytics, automated reporting, and cloud sync are available under the premium plan. Visit bm.lumenaautomation.co.in for full details.
Do you offer post-deployment support?
Yes. All enterprise engagements include a post-deployment support period. We also offer ongoing managed support and SLA-backed monitoring packages. Reach out at support@lumenaautomation.co.in for support queries.
Can you integrate with our existing systems?
Absolutely. Our custom software and API development capabilities allow seamless integration with most existing enterprise systems, CRMs, ERPs, and third-party platforms. We specialize in bridging legacy infrastructure with modern cloud architecture.
