How to Use BudgetManager

Master your money management in just a few simple steps

Published: October 2025 • By LumenaAutomation

💡 What Is BudgetManager?

BudgetManager is your smart companion for personal finance. It helps you plan monthly budgets, track daily expenses, and visualize where your money goes — all through an intuitive and ad-free dashboard.

🏁 Step 1: Create Your Account

  • Visit LumenaAutomation.co.in and open the BudgetManager section.
  • Sign up with your name, email, and password.
  • Log in to access your personalized dashboard.

Tip: You can update your profile anytime with new details like email or phone.

💰 Step 2: Set Your Monthly Budget

Enter your total monthly amount and distribute it across categories like Grocery, Medical, Entertainment, Transport, Education, Bills, Food, Shelter, Savings, and Others.

  • Add custom categories unique to your lifestyle.
  • Live progress bars show how your total adds up.
  • Warnings appear if your allocation exceeds the total budget.

Bonus: Select ready-made budget plans such as Basic, Balanced, or 50-30-20 to auto-allocate your categories.

🧾 Step 3: Add Daily Expenses

Quickly record what you spend each day. The app automatically assigns expenses to main categories to keep your reports accurate and clean.

📊 Step 4: Analyze Your Dashboard

See a clear picture of your finances:

  • Interactive donut charts showing category-wise spending
  • Monthly bar charts comparing budgeted vs. actual
  • Smart alerts for overspending

📅 Step 5: Track Yearly Trends

Switch to the Trends view to explore how your spending and budgeting patterns evolve month by month. Spot improvement areas and fine-tune your plan.

⚙️ Step 6: Customize Settings

Change your language, currency, and budget plans — all from one page. Your choices stay saved across sessions and devices.

📱 Step 7: Use on the Go

BudgetManager is optimized for Android and mobile browsers. Add expenses, view reports, or download PDFs directly from your phone.

🎯 Why You’ll Love BudgetManager

  • Simple, intuitive design
  • Secure and private — your data stays yours
  • Insightful analytics and charts
  • Completely ad-free and free to use
