How to Use BudgetManager — Master Your Money in 7 Simple Steps

October 2025 6 min read LumenaAutomation Personal Finance, Product

What Is BudgetManager?

BudgetManager is LumenaAutomation's flagship personal finance platform — a smart, ad-free companion that helps you plan monthly budgets, track daily expenses, and visualize exactly where your money goes. Built for students, professionals, and small business owners alike, it replaces spreadsheets with intelligent automation and beautiful charts.

Why BudgetManager stands out: No ads, no clutter — just clean financial intelligence. Completely free to get started, with secure cloud sync across all your devices.

The 7-Step Guide

1

Create Your Account

Visit bm.lumenaautomation.co.in and sign up with your name, email, and password. Once logged in, your personalized dashboard is ready immediately — no setup wizard, no confusion.

2

Set Your Monthly Budget

Enter your total monthly income and distribute it across categories: Grocery, Medical, Entertainment, Transport, Education, Bills, Food, Shelter, Savings, and Others. Add custom categories unique to your lifestyle. Live progress bars warn you instantly if allocations exceed your total.

Quick Start: Use built-in budget plan templates — Basic, Balanced, or 50-30-20 — to auto-allocate categories in one click. No manual math required.

3

Add Daily Expenses

Record what you spend each day in seconds. BudgetManager automatically assigns expenses to the correct main category — keeping your reports accurate and your tracking effortless, even on the busiest days.

4

Analyze Your Dashboard

Your dashboard gives you the full financial picture instantly — interactive donut charts show category-wise spending, monthly bar charts compare budgeted vs. actual spend, and smart alerts fire before you overspend in any category.

5

Track Yearly Trends

Switch to the Trends view to explore how your spending and budgeting patterns evolve month by month across the year. Spot improvement areas, identify savings opportunities, and fine-tune your plan with real data.

6

Customize Your Settings

Change your language, currency, and default budget plan — all from one settings page. Your preferences persist automatically across sessions and devices. BudgetManager supports multiple currencies for global users.

7

Use On the Go

BudgetManager is fully optimized for Android and mobile browsers. Add expenses, review reports, or download PDF summaries directly from your phone — your finances travel with you wherever you go.

Why You'll Love BudgetManager

  • Completely ad-free — your financial space stays clean and focused
  • Intelligent automation — AI categorization means less manual work every day
  • Visual-first design — beautiful charts that make sense of your money instantly
  • Privacy-first architecture — your data is yours, secured with encryption
  • Zero learning curve — intuitive from your very first session

Pro Tip: Try the Zero-Based Budget plan inside BudgetManager to assign every rupee a specific purpose — ensuring you always know exactly where your money is going and never lose track of savings goals.
